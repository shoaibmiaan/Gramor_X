User Module

Auth & Identity Profile & Onboarding Study Plan Subscriptions Notifications Security & Privacy

Core

  • Authentication: Email/Password, Phone OTP (Twilio), Google, Apple, Facebook; magic link; password reset.
  • Roles: student / teacher / admin with route guards and feature gating.
  • Onboarding: collect full name, country, phone, goal band, exam date, weaknesses, language.
  • Profile: avatar, bio, timezone, language & theme preferences, accessibility options.

Learning & Personalization

  • Study Plan: daily/weekly targets, ETA to goal, auto-adjust plan from performance.
  • Dashboard: streaks, today’s tasks, weak-skill drills, recent attempts, AI coach tips.
  • Progress: attempt history, band trajectory, accuracy by type, time-on-task, downloadable reports.
  • Saved: bookmarks, flagged questions, retake queue, vocabulary & grammar lists.

Engagement

  • Notifications: reminders (study sessions, test dates), email/SMS/WhatsApp, quiet hours.
  • Calendar: study calendar with rescheduling, missed-session catchup, Google/Apple export.
  • Referral: referral link, credits/rewards, invite tracking.

Billing & Access

  • Subscriptions: plan management (Compass/Seedling/Rocket/Owl), upgrade/downgrade, invoices & receipts.
  • Entitlements: feature gating per plan, soft nudges for locked features.

Security & Data

  • Account Security: 2FA (email/OTP), active sessions/devices, login history, revoke device.
  • Privacy & Data: profile export, delete account, communications preferences, GDPR-ready.
  • Support: help center, contact, issue reporter with logs (consented).

  • Motivation AI Coach (/coach): mood/energy‑aware micro‑nudges, pep‑talks, and weekly reflections based on engagement patterns (privacy‑first; no sensitive inference stored).
  • Slip‑Recovery Autopilot (/study-plan): if tasks are missed, auto‑rebalances the next 7 days with lighter “restart” sessions and confidence‑builder drills.
  • 10‑Minute Mode (/quick): one‑tap “I have 10 minutes” that serves a focused micro‑drill from your weakest skill with instant feedback.
  • Noise Ladder for Listening (/labs/noise-ladder): progressive background noise & accents to harden real‑world comprehension.
  • Accent Mirror for Speaking (/labs/accent-mirror): shadowing + alignment meter and minimal‑pairs trainer targeting your specific pronunciation gaps.
  • Exam‑Day Rehearsal Suite (/exam-day): device/mic check, timing rehearsal, anxiety‑control scripts, and a dry‑run checklist.
  • Study‑Buddy Match (/study-buddy): timezone & goal‑band matching for 15‑minute speaking swaps with built‑in prompts and timers.
  • “My Mistakes” Book (/my-mistakes): auto‑compiled grammar/vocab errors from attempts → spaced practice with examples rewritten in your context.
  • Challenge‑the‑Grade (/review/challenge): ask “why?” on AI scores; see rubric‑based justification and evidence highlights.
  • Parent/Coach Share (/share): read‑only progress link with weekly summaries and upcoming plan.
  • WhatsApp Daily Tasks (/notifications): opt‑in task pings and quick‑reply submissions via WhatsApp/SMS.
  • Language‑of‑Explanation (/settings/language): AI feedback/explanations in the user’s preferred language, examples localized.
  • Offline‑Lite PWA (/offline): cache practice sets, vocab, and drafts; sync when back online.
  • Context‑Aware Calendar (/study-plan/calendar): auto‑lighten during travel/festivals/exams; catch‑up windows later.
  • Visa/Admissions Readiness (/readiness): track target scores per university/visa, show gap‑to‑goal and deadlines.
  • Streak Shield (/streak): limited “sick‑day” tokens to protect long streaks without breaking habit psychology.
