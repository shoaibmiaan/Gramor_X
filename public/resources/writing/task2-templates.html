IELTS Writing — Task 2 Templates

Templates guide structure, not thinking. Adjust to the exact question.

Opinion (Agree/Disagree)

Intro: Paraphrase + clear thesis (I agree / I disagree).

Body 1 (reason 1): Topic sentence → explain → brief example → mini-conclusion.

Body 2 (reason 2): Topic sentence → explain → brief example → mini-conclusion.

Conclusion: Restate position + strongest reason.

Thesis frame: “I agree that … because A, and B.”

Discuss Both Views (+ Opinion)

Intro: Paraphrase + state you will discuss both; give your view.

Body 1: Explain View A fairly; example; mini-conclusion.

Body 2: Explain View B fairly; example; mini-conclusion.

Conclusion: State which is more convincing and why.

Opinion line: “While A offers …, B is stronger because …”

Advantages / Disadvantages

Intro: Paraphrase + overall stance (which side outweighs).

Body 1: Advantages (one strong, one supporting).

Body 2: Disadvantages (address and evaluate).

Conclusion: On balance, which side is stronger.

Problem / Solution

Intro: Paraphrase + outline of causes/solutions.

Body 1: Causes (prioritise 1–2 real drivers).

Body 2: Solutions (feasible, targeted; who will do what, when).

Conclusion: Coordinated measures beat isolated steps.

Concession + Rebuttal (1-liner)

“Although X may …, a well-designed Y achieves … which ultimately …”
