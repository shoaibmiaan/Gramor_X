IELTS Writing — Linking Words Bank

Use sparingly. Quality > quantity. Let logic lead; linkers support.

Add / Sequence

  • moreover, furthermore, in addition, also
  • first, next, finally

Contrast

  • however, nevertheless, even so
  • while, whereas, although

Cause → Effect

  • therefore, consequently, as a result
  • thus, hence

Examples

  • for example, for instance
  • such as, namely

Emphasis

  • indeed, notably, in fact
  • in particular, especially

Condition

  • if, provided that, unless
  • as long as

Comparison

  • similarly, likewise
  • in contrast, on the other hand

Concession

  • admittedly, although, even though
  • granted (that)

Conclusion

  • overall, in sum, to conclude
  • ultimately, on balance
